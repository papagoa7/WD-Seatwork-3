ABOUT THE COMPANY

Freddy Fazbear's Pizza is a pizzeria set up by Fazbear Entertainment
open from 1988 to 1993. Freddy Fazbear's Pizza at night is a dimly
light restaurant. The place is covered in posters with the characters
saying various things such as "Play, Party, Eat", in addition, the place
is covered in pizza decorations and slices of pizza and black and
white tiled room and other various decorations scattered very poorly
around the establishment.
